Privacy Policy

App Name: SimpleTorch

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SimpleTorch ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

SimpleTorch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline and does not require user accounts, sign-ups, or personal data.

2. Permissions Used by the App

The app requires the following permissions strictly for functionality:

Camera Permission

  • android.permission.CAMERA
  • android.hardware.camera

This permission allows the app to access the device camera hardware in order to control the flashlight (camera flash). The app does not take photos, record videos, or access camera images.

Flashlight Permission

  • android.permission.FLASHLIGHT

This permission allows the app to turn the device flashlight on and off.
